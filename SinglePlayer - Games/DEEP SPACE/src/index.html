DEEP SPACE

START

You are travelling back to Earth.

After 15 years of hyper sleep, you awaken to find your ship has drifted off course, and is heavily damaged. Other than critical life support systems, very few components aboard the ship are operational.

You are orbiting an unknown planet, and with no communication network, you have no way of knowing where you are or how to get home.

Your aim is to begin repairing the ships components and ultimately repair your communication network to contact Earth for rescue.

BEGIN

By using the repaired communication network and large satellite network you have developed, an SOS message was sent to Earth.

You receive a reply which contains information about the current planet and system you are in, as well as a flight plan to return home.

As the ship begins it's first orbital transfer, you prepare for hyper sleep, wondering if you'll make it home this time.
